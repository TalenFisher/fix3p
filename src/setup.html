FiX3P

Setup Instructions

Just a few more steps to setup the google chrome fix3p extension!

Step 1.

Type chrome://extensions into your URL bar and press enter.

Step 2.

Locate the FiX3P extension and click on details.

Step 3.

Locate the Allow access to file URLs setting an make sure it is enabled.

Good to go!

You can now open X3P files in chrome and it will automatically load in FiX3P!
